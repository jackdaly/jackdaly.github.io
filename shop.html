CNC Dust Shoe

CNC Dust shoes are incredibly important when milling any material, especially materials that can be toxic or bad for the user's health. With this project, I aimed to combine functionality with aesthetics to create a highly functional product that looks aesthetically pleasing. The project was designed in Fusion 360 and showcases how highly functional tools can also take on pleasing form. The shoe can be moved up and down, adjusted with ease and the hoover extension can be removed for different additions.

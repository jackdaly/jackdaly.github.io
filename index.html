ABOUT ME

Hybrid Designer, programmer and ginger.

Jack Daly


I label myself as a hybrid designer, but what does that mean? Well, I have a wide range of skills, ranging from making and programming robots and websites to graphic and product design - with both CAD & CAM knowledge sandwiched in between them. I love designing products and have the ability to integrate all 3 pillars of design - resistant materials, graphics and electronics.

I'm a 16-year-old ginger. During the day I am hiding in the shade at school studying and at night I'm tinkering in my workshop doing personal and freelance projects.
